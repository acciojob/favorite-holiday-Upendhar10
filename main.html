Sikkim - Gangtok

Incredibly alluring, pleasantly boisterous and wreathed in clouds – Gangtok, the capital of Sikkim, is one of the most popular hill stations in India. Lying at the height of 1650 m above sea level, the town offers spectacular views of Mt. Kanchenjunga on clear days. It serves as a base for adventure lovers, especially trekkers and campers exploring the Himalayan ranges.

  1. Natula Pass – A historic Indo-China mountain pass
  2. MG Marg – The central shopping and food street
  3. Tsomgo Lake – A glacial lake surrounded by snow-capped peaks
  4. Rumtek Monastery – A spiritual and architectural marvel
  5. Lachung – A beautiful mountain village in North Sikkim
  • Momos – Steamed dumplings loved by locals and tourists
  • Thukpa – A hearty noodle soup
  • Gundruk – A fermented leafy green vegetable dish
  • Sel Roti with Aloo Dum – A traditional Nepali rice bread combo
  • Chhurpi Soup – A soup made with hardened yak cheese
